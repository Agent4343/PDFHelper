        Search Documents

        What does this do? Find things inside your PDFs — like using Ctrl+F but across ALL your documents at once. Two ways to search:

        • Keyword Search — Type exact words and it finds every place those words appear
        • AI Search — Describe what you're looking for in everyday language (like "anything about safety rules") and the AI finds related content, even if it uses different words

        Multi-Agent Analysis

        What does this do? This sends your documents to 4 AI helpers that each check for different things:

        1. Document Analyzer — Reads each document and figures out what it's about
        2. Cross-Reference Checker — Compares your documents to find things that disagree with each other
        3. Compliance Checker — Checks if anything is missing, outdated, or doesn't follow the rules
        4. Summary Report — Writes a final report telling you what needs to be fixed, starting with the most important things

        Pick which documents to analyze, optionally enter a compliance standard, then click Run Analysis.

        Load documents first from the Documents tab

        History & Reports

        What does this do? This saves everything you've done before — like a diary for the app. You can look back at old searches you ran and old analysis reports without having to run them again.

        PDF Tools

        What does this do? These are tools to change and edit your PDFs — like scissors, glue, and a marker for your documents:

        • Merge — Glue multiple PDFs together into one big file
        • Split — Cut out specific pages and save them as a new file
        • Add Text — Write words on top of a page (like stamping "DRAFT" or adding a note)

        Merge PDFs

        Pick 2 or more documents. They'll be glued together in the order shown.

        Split / Extract Pages

        Pick a document and choose which pages you want to pull out. They'll be saved as a brand new file. Use commas for individual pages (1,3,5) or dashes for ranges (1-5).

        Add Text / Watermark

        Write text on top of any page — like stamping "DRAFT" or "CONFIDENTIAL", or adding a note. Pick where on the page it goes, how big, and what color.

        IsoIntel — Isolation Package Generator

        What does this do? This is a special tool for people who work on oil rigs, platforms, or industrial plants. When you need to shut down a piece of equipment safely (called "isolation"), this tool:

        1. Looks at your engineering drawings (P&ID diagrams)
        2. Figures out all the pipes, valves, and energy sources connected to the equipment
        3. Writes a step-by-step safety procedure telling workers exactly which valves to close, what to lock out, and in what order

        If you don't work in this industry, you can skip this tab entirely.

        P&ID Drawings

        Upload your P&ID drawings (PDF or images). The AI reads these to trace flow paths and identify isolation points.
